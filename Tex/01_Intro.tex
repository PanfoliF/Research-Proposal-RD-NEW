\section{Introduction}
In this article, I aim to examine the educational dynamics of Brazil, with a particular focus on effects of schools quality on national tests scores.
The Brazilian case offers a compelling context for analysis.

\vspace{1em}
The author aims to study the impact of the institutional settings on educational quality variables. The author will use the introduction of different laws and use them to assess the impact on variables that describe the marital and educational status of women.

\vspace{1em}
This project uses the Brazilian School Panel to study how teacher quality shapes student learning and school progression.

\vspace*{1em}
My goal is to distinguish two channels: standardized test performance (\textbf{extensive margin}) and grade progression outcomes (\textbf{intensive margin}).
